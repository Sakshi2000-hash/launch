Features

Designed with you in mind.

Premium materials

Kaya is designed to blend in beautifully with your home. All the fabrics and materials have been carefully selected for aesthetics and the best audio performance.

Durable construction

We made sure Kaya outlasts all the curious friends. The cloth is made from hemp fiber crafted by Tanzanian artisans. The inside is built with industrial graphite-light and powerful. The base is made of African wood, sourced carefully from local cooperatives.

USB connectivity

Play your music directly from your device through the elegant USB entry port. It’s easy to connect with any USB device.

Ring light

See the status of your Kaya with the simple lights on the crown of your device. It’ll help you know when your smart speaker is pairing, listening, thinking, and speaking. When you want to turn your speaker off, simply hold the Kaya logo LED button.

Made to work together

Not only do all 3 Kaya speakers look and feel like a family, they also work together. Play music across all the speakers around the house with just a few words.

LED main button

The Kaya logo on the top is an indicator of the battery level. It also provides a nice presence into the room while a long press will turn the device on or off. During pairing bars of the logo indicate the wifi signal intensity.
